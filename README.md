# Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive overview of sales performance across regions, countries, products, product categories, time periods, and salespersons. It enables users to quickly analyze revenue trends, sales distribution, and key contributors to overall performance.

---

## Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue (5M)**  
  Displays the total sales revenue generated from all transactions.

- **Total Units Sold (2K)**  
  Shows the total number of units sold across all products and regions.

- **Average Unit Price (556K)**  
  Represents the average price per unit calculated from total revenue and total units sold.

---

## Dashboard Visuals

### Revenue Analysis
- **Revenue by Region**  
  A bar chart comparing total revenue generated in Europe, Asia, and Africa.

- **Revenue by Country**  
  A horizontal bar chart showing revenue contribution from individual countries such as India, the UK, Germany, and Uganda.

- **Revenue by Product**  
  Displays revenue generated by each product, highlighting top-performing items.

- **Revenue by Product Category**  
  A donut chart illustrating how revenue is distributed across Electronics, Furniture, and Stationery categories.

### Sales Performance
- **Units Sold by Salesperson**  
  Shows the number of units sold by each salesperson, helping identify high performers.

- **Revenue by Month**  
  A line chart tracking monthly revenue trends to identify growth patterns and seasonal variations.

---

## Sales Details Table
- Displays detailed sales information including:
  - Month
  - Product
  - Region
  - Salesperson
  - Revenue
- Includes a total revenue summary at the bottom of the table.

---

## Interactive Filters
- **Region Filter** – Filters the dashboard by selected region.
- **Product Category Filter** – Allows analysis by specific product categories.
- **Quarter Filter (Qtr 1–Qtr 4)** – Enables time-based analysis by quarter.

All visuals update dynamically based on selected filters.

---

## Purpose
This dashboard demonstrates practical use of Power BI for:
- Data cleaning and modeling
- Interactive visualization
- Sales performance analysis
- Supporting data-driven decision making
